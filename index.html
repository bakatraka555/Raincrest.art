Frequently Asked Questions

How does the transformation work? ▼
You upload a clear face photo, choose your scenes (King or Queen variant), and our AI transforms you into medieval royalty using the real Game of Thrones filming locations in Dubrovnik. You'll receive high-res photos and cinematic videos within 24 hours.
Do I need to travel to Dubrovnik? ▼
No! Everything is done digitally. We use AI to place you in Dubrovnik's iconic locations. Just upload your photo and we handle the rest. Perfect for fans who can't travel to Croatia.
What if I don't like the result? ▼
We offer 100% money-back guarantee within 7 days. Plus free revisions per order (1 for Basic, 2 for Popular, unlimited for Royal) to ensure you're completely satisfied with your royal transformation.
How long does delivery take? ▼
Standard delivery is 24 hours for Basic, 18 hours for Popular (priority), and 12 hours for Royal (rush). All transformations are manually reviewed for quality before delivery. You'll receive an email with download links.
Can I use this for social media? ▼
Absolutely! Your photos and videos are fully licensed for personal use, including social media. Tag us @raincrest.art and use #ClaimYourThrone for a chance to be featured on our page!
